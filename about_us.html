About Us

Our Bakery produces delectable pastries that combine classic asian traditions with modern flavors for the contemporary palette. Here one can sample a plethora of bakery and confectionery options in terms of different variety of breads, cookies, chocolates, cakes, pies, tarts, muffins and pastries. Additionally, one can also taste a wide range of mouth watering snacks including pasta, pizza, sandwiches, wraps, rolls and burgers. In addition, we can fulfill your catering and wedding needs with a dessert menu of creamy delicious cakes, chocolates and cookies. Our Bakery prides itself on the personalized service it offers to each of its wedding cake and special occasion cake clients. Our decorators use only the freshest and finest ingredients available.

Our Story

Finest in quality, this scrumptious venture was brought in the year 2006 to the City of Lakes by Mr. Sanjay, Mr. Girish and Mr. Vinod Talreja who wanted to share their passion for food. It is this sustained commitment to excellence backed by a world class production, distribution network and customer friendly service that has enabled us to carve a niche for ourselves across the length and breadth of Bhopal. The secret of our excellence lies in the meticulous care and detailed craft that goes into each and every one of our products. Today, Bake n shake is a household name in every nook and corner of Bhopal & Indore. At present, we have 5 successful running outlets, out of which 3 are in Bhopal and 2 outlets in Indore each one contributing the same quality and flavors, with only one goal in mind: The pleasure of your palate!
